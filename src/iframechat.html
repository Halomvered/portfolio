    Mini project overview:
    The script's canvas particles react to your mouse movements and clicks by changing their colors.

    The more your mouse stays or move through a certain area, the 'hotter' the colors of that area become. While clicks are represented by the color purple.


    Move the mouse around the page, and when you're ready generate the heat map, or see the script works in real time: press play.
