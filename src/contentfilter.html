    Mini project overview:
    This script is designed to collect the content of the meta
    tags: "Keywords", and "Description", alongside all of the headers, and
    image's "alt".

    It then filters commonly used transition words out of them, finds the four
    most repeated keywords, and injects them into a recommended
    content pop-up.


    To employ the script press the play button.
